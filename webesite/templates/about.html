WHAT IS ECOTRACK?

Welcome to Ecotrack, the cutting-edge waste management system revolutionizing sustainable practices in Batangas City. Our platform offers a seamless solution for junk shops and individuals, enabling easy tracking of users and waste drop-off locations while empowering users to manage their waste generation, disposal, and recycling habits with precision and efficiency. Join us in our mission to transform waste management and promote sustainable development.
